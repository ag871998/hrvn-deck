Why We Exist

Vision & Mission

"Successful people who have relocated to Mallorca often have everything money can buy but feel disconnected. HRVN creates the belonging layer that money alone cannot provide."
Vision

A haven for the human behind the success.

A private luxury members club in Puerto Portals — a handpicked community of global families built on real friendship, shared experience and Interest Circles, where members are finally seen, find their people, and reconnect with purpose.

Mission

Curate belonging, not membership.

Money alone doesn't open the door — admission is by conversation, recommendation and a real vote. Accepted members then pay a membership fee. You earn the room; you don't simply buy your way in.

NOT a networking club NOT a gentleman's club NOT a Soho House NOT a mass-market hub IS a private luxury members club IS handpicked & curated IS membership by fee & invitation IS real friendship & shared experience
